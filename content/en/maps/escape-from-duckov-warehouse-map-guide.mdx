## High-Tier Loot Locations on the Escape from Duckov Warehouse Map

Finding loot on the **escape from duckov warehouse map** requires knowing where to look. It’s not just about random crates; specific shelves hold specific items.

### The Foreman’s Office
Located on the second floor overlooking the main floor, this room is a hotspot on the **escape from duckov warehouse map**. You will need the **Foreman’s Key**. Inside, you can find **Intelligence Folders** and **Gold Bars**.

### The Shipping Container Maze
In the center of the **escape from duckov warehouse map**, there is a stack of blue containers. If you have high **Agility** from your **Perk Palace**, you can parkour up here.

**"The Forklift"** patrols the central area of the **escape from duckov warehouse map**. He is dangerous because he has three guards who will flank you. To defeat him, do not fight in the open. Lure him into the narrow corridors of the **escape from duckov warehouse map** where his numbers don't matter.
